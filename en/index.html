About Me

I am a software developer from California in the United States.

Programming

I'm most interested in software and hardware development based around the concept of functional reactive streams. At some point, I would like to develop hardware and a corresponding operating system that lends itself easily to fs2- and cats-effect-like programming. I believe that those frameworks lend themselves to easier understanding of programs as well as higher maintainability, but most important to me is the environmental impact.

I'm quite interested in energy-efficient computing, having my eyes on companies like GreenArrays, who are developing clockless circuits built to be energy-efficient, and technology like the Low-tech Magazine, a blog entirely powered by solar. In conjunction with low-power computing is making the internet more accessible and less costly. One of the reasons this website is so bare-bones is because I want it to be navigable in low-cost browsers such as Lynx and EWW.

As for programming accomplishments, and not aspirations, I currently mainly program in Scala, and am very proficient in Java. Other languages I am able to program easily in include C, C++, F#, Kotlin, and Zig. I have experience writing Rust, TypeScript/JavaScript, Python, Crystal, and Haskell, but I would not consider myself skilled enough to write programs easily in them. My GitHub URL at the moment is s5bug, but this is likely to change. Unfortunately, my project descriptions are only available in English.

Soon, I would like to be able to be comfortable making games in Godot, as well as SmileBASIC 4. I have been wanting to make something substantial in SmileBASIC 4 for a long time, but haven't learned how to use it correctly yet.

Music

The two most-played musical instruments in my possession are my flute (a Yamaha YFL-200ADII) and my AKAI EWI Solo. I very much like playing jazz as well as video game music. At some point, I'd like to learn how to use a DAW so that I can make full pieces playing on my own instruments. Other instruments I have access to include an acoustic guitar (unknown model) and a clarinet (unknown model).

Mathematics

Recently, I have become more interested in category and type theory. The main reason for my interest in these types of math is the ways in which they can be applied to the aspects of software and hardware development I care about. Currently, I'm interested in solidifying my understanding of category theory by writing compilers from complex languages into toy languages like Fancade.

Gaming

Playing video games is my go-to past-time. My favorite games so far are, in no particular order, Celeste, Marble Saga: Kororinpa, Mario Kart Wii, and The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time. I've completed numerous Ocarina of Time randomizers. I very much enjoy Ocarina of Time Multiworld randomizers, as they allow me to spend time having fun with my friends. I don't have to focus on the game at all times, but it is still an incredibly enjoyable mental challenge.

Currently I'm in the process of playing through a Minecraft 1.18 world with my friends, as well as progressing through my first playthrough of Hollow Knight.

About This Website

The entirety of aly.fish is supposed to be easily browsable without JavaScript, CSS, or custom fonts. CSS and custom fonts are used to make the website more consistent, and may be loaded by your browser. On certain links without a language in the URL (for example, this page has /en/), JavaScript will be loaded to redirect to the user's preferred language. If the user has JavaScript disabled, a list of languages that the page is available in will be shown.
